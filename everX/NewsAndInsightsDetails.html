Saving 40 Hours a Week and Fueling Rapid Growth for Kudos Diapers

October 24, 2024

Challenge

Kudos is a rapidly growing diaper brand. Chief of Staff Ilan Tagger struggled with time-consuming manual processes, including demand forecasting in Excel, managing purchase orders, shipments, and controlling inventory levels and logistics. This led to frequent stockouts and over 40 hours a week of wasted time.

Solution

Terra is an AI operator that provides Kudos end-to-end inventory management. It accurately forecasts demand, optimizes inventory and manages logistics. Terra significantly reduces manual work, allowing Kudos to maintain a small team while continuing its rapid growth.

Benefits

Peace of Mind

Ilan Tagger, Chief of Staff, can focus on strategy and growth with complete peace of mind, while Terra works seamlessly in the background, managing inventory in its entirety.

Cash Flow Management

Terra ensures a healthy cash flow by maintaining inventory levels that align with Kudo's cash capabilities. With Terra, Kudos always has the right amount of stock at the right time and place,preventing both stockouts and overstocks

Improved Visibility

Terra provides valuable insights into supplier performance, ensuring that delivery times are met and preventing inventory shrinkage.
