How it works

Terra controls, plans, moves, and optimizes your inventory to help you maintain 98% in-stock levels with lean inventory—at a fraction of the cost of building an in-house supply chain team.

read more

DROPSHIPPING

Maintain 98% in stock with lean inventory between 30-90 days.

Terra efficiently moves your products directly from manufacturers to customers. For brands manufacturing offshore or outside the U.S., we store your inventory near your factories and handle fulfillment and shipping directly to your customers' doorsteps. This approach enables you to maintain lean inventory while reducing fulfillment and shipping costs by 30%.

  • Storage management
  • Inventory management Pick and pack services
  • Inventory managementDirect shipping from country of origin to end customer’s doorstep

LOGISTICS MANAGEMENT

Gain full visibility of your supply chain without breaking a sweat.

Terra acts as the GPS for your products, ensuring you have the right products at the right place at the right time. Say goodbye to stockouts and overstocking. everx predictive inventory management navigates the daily challenges to ensure everything remains balanced

  • Sales Forecasting: predicts future sales accurately.
  • Direct shipping from country of origin to end customer’s doorstep
  • Replenishments: manages your purchasing and inbound shipments
  • Optimal Placement: ensures to stock the right inventory level at every location.
  • Freight shipping: ships inventory both domestically and across borders directly to your 3PL doorstep, ensuring smooth transit from the factory to the fulfillment cente
  • Logistics Management: Reconciliation, Delivery Monitoring, 3PL Oversight and more.
